About

As Professor of AI in Remote Sensing at [Stuttgart University of Applied Sciences](https://www.hft-stuttgart.com/), I teach and research at the intersection of machine learning/deep learning and remote sensing. My motivation is to find efficient implementations for interdisciplinary problems involving remote sensing data of different modalities.
